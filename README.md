- To start a new project, select **File > New** inside the software.  
- To open an existing model, go to **File > Open** and choose your saved file.  
- Save your work regularly by clicking **File > Save** or **Save As** to create new versions.  
- Models are saved as .ZPR files by default, but you can export to other formats for use in other programs.

## 🖱 How to Sculpt: The Basics

1. Select a brush from the toolbar on the left side.  
2. Use your mouse or tablet pen to click and drag on the model surface.  
3. Hold specific keys while brushing to smooth, inflate, or move parts of your model (check the software’s help for key assignments).  
4. Use the zoom and rotate controls on the right to view your model from different angles.  
5. Undo any change by pressing **Ctrl + Z**.  
6. Experiment by layering details and textures for realistic results.
